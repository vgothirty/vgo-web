What is VGO?

VGO is a global payment system built on point-based system where users earn the points through work done on the startups for their product development, marketing and support and use these points to pay utility bills, to do shopping and any other bill payments wherever these points are acceptable to make the payment across the globe. The value of these points grow as the startups generate more sales.

How it works?

When an entrepreneur posts an idea in VGO center to build and support his or her startup, VGO center assigns points based on its share price, generates the new points and puts them on hold. This is to ensure that these new points won’t disrupt the existing value of VGO unit (1 VGO unit = 1000 points). Once the startup goes into sales, points are released in less or equal number to the growth in the value of VGO unit. This will ensure that there is stable, steady and organic growth in the value of VGO unit and most importantly no more drop in its value. Points are then distributed to partners when they complete the work on the startup.

  • Generates new points when a new startup arrives.
  • Releases points when the startup goes into sales.
  • Distributes points to partners for building, marketing and supporting the upcoming startups.
